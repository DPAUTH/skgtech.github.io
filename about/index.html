About Us

SKGTech is a non profit organization comprised of creative and passionate people that love all things tech. We organize Local Meetups, International Conferences and create Apps and Services to aid the community at large.

We care about a thriving, growing, self-sustainable environment where teams, individuals, businesses and all kinds of actors can benefit from.
