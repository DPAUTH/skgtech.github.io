About SKGTech

SKGTech is a Non Profit Organization comprised of all kinds of technology friends and professionals. Our mission is to help local and global communities in leveraging technologies to improve their quality of living.

We are all volunteers, each one of us doing what we know best to produce well crafted and impactful events, projects, campaigns and fundraisers. For a list of our work you can checkout the Projects page
